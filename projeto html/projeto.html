This is the best cookies recipe ever

What you will need

The recipe is easy, and you will need 8 ingredients.

  • Butter - 180g
  • Brown Suggar - 120g
  • White Suggar - 90g
  • 1 large egg
  • Baking Powder - 5g
  • Flour - 250g
  • Vanilla Extract - 5ml
  • Chopped chocolate or chocolate chips - 180g

Note: the first step is optional and link video below is to help you if necessary.
